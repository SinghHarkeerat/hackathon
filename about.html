About Us

About Us & Our Goal

In our increasingly diverse and inclusive world, we believe that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their communication preferences or abilities. That’s why we’ve integrated a state-of-the-art Sign Language Detection Model into our platform. Our sign language detection model is a groundbreaking tool that bridges the gap between the hearing and the deaf communities in the educational sector. It works by accurately recognizing and interpreting sign language in real-time, thereby enabling seamless communication and interaction.
